You walk through the dark, eerie forest, moonlight shining down from the canopy. Each step you take, you hear the cracking of branches breaking beneath your feet. You hear a wolf's howl.

Suddenly, you catch sight of a shadowy figure in the distance. It appears to be an old woman, dressed in her nightgown, her figure slightly obscured by the dim light.

As you get closer, preparing to call out, you notice something strange. Tufts of fur protruding from her gown and two sharp eyes looking at you from the darkness. You hold your breath as the silent forest goes still.

"Ah, good day, young child. Where are you off to this fine evening?" the figure says in a low and gruff voice.

"I-I am lost", you reply shakily. "Might you be so kind as to help me find my way back home?"

"I could help you, child, but there is a price for my kindness." The stranger steps closer, their figure illuminated by the moonlight. You gasp - sharp claws where fingers should be, a grin revealing large yellow fangs.

"I shall help you, but only if you answer me five questions. But if you fail..." The wolf smiles, licking its lips. "I shall have you for dinner. Do you accept?"
